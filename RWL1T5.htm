Short Answer
 
 
INSTRUCTIONS: Combine the two sentences into one sentence with "whereas." Use correct punctuation.
5-1
 

 1. 
In Chinese cooking, soy sauce is considered a staple at most meals. Americans just use soy sauce occasionally.
 
 

 2. 
Most of the time, the Chinese serve rice in small bowls. Americans usually serve rice in a big bowl or on the main plate.
 
 

 3. 
In general, the Chinese serve meat in small portions. Americans serve meat in large portions, such as steaks and hamburgers.
 
 

 4. 
It is the custom for Americans to eat with a knife and a fork. Most of the time, the Chinese eat their food with chopsticks.
 
 

 5. 
It is not common for American restaurants to serve duck. In Chinese cuisine, duck is often considered a special meal when families go out to eat.
