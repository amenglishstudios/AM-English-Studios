"Diego Rivera"

     Have you ever heard of Diego Rivera? Diego Rivera, Frida Kahlo's husband, was a popular artist well known for his murals: large paintings on or in public buildings. The Mexican Revolution of 1911 influenced Mexican muralists at this time, and they painted murals that expressed their feelings about the current social and political conditions. Like his wife Frida, Rivera's work was unconventional and not always accepted by the public.
