What is Success?

     Although the meaning of the word "success" varies from culture to culture, generally it refers to some form of achievement. It is also considered a positive goal to attain. After immigrating to the U.S. ten years ago, I have had many opportunities to learn that the definition of success in the U.S. is different than in my home country. In the United States, success is often defined as material success, a high education and a happy family life.
