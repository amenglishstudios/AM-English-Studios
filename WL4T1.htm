INSTRUCTIONS: Read the first and last paragraph of this essay. Complete an outline for this essay. Use the existing paragraphs, your knowledge of the subject, and your imagination to complete the outline.

First Paragraph:
          Have you ever experienced severe culture shock? When a person moves into a new culture, there are significant societal differences that must be acknowledged. The newcomer must understand the effects these changes will cause as well as the new behaviors that are required to respond to unfamiliar cultural cues. The importance of adaptation cannot be underestimated since the success or failure of a trip or living situation in a foreign environment depends on this process. There are certain steps one can take to successfully move between and adapt to a new culture.

Last Paragraph:
          There is no doubt about it; culture shock creates psychological, physical and emotional stress that wouldn't occur for a person staying at home. Therefore, adaptation is necessary. Learning to adjust is critically important. There are steps that can be taken to insure a high level of accomplishment. Many travelers can attest to the wisdom of preparing themselves before leaving home culture. If the steps are followed carefully, successful adaptation to the host culture will almost always occur.
