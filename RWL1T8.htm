A Meeting of Mice

     A long time ago, in the town of Ulm, the village mice gathered to solve a serious problem. The mice lived in constant fear of the village cat. The cat's favorite activity was playing with a mouse and then eating it up. The mice had been discussing possible solutions for a long time, but nothing seemed like a good idea.

     Suddenly, a young mouse called from the back.

     "Wait! I have an idea! Let's put a bell around the cat's neck. Whenever the cat gets near us, we will hear the cat coming and run and hide."

     The mice in the room jumped up excitedly and applauded! They thought it was a great idea! But soon one of the older, wiser mice asked for an opportunity to speak. He said that for sure, a bell around the cat's neck would be a signal that the cat is coming.

     "But," he asked, "Which mouse would put the bell around the cat's neck?"
