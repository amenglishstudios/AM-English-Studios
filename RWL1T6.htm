Native American Languages

     Did you know that there are over 500 Native American Indian Tribes in the U.S. and over 100 Native languages? Native Americans have a rich cultural heritage, and the language of each tribe reflects the unique quality that identifies each community. However, not everyone in each tribe is able to read or speak his or her native language. Also, the younger generation thinks their native language is old-fashioned and they don't want to study it.
